Programs

Goalie Academy offers age-aware training for U7-U20 goalies, from first crease habits to advanced game-read skills.

Each program combines skating, positioning, save selection, rebound control, puck handling, and confidence under pressure.

Book a Session

Camps & Intensives

Short-format training blocks for goalies who want focused reps, feedback, and measurable progress.

Holiday Camps

Multi-day on-ice sessions with goalie-specific drills, movement patterns, and daily coaching points.

Weekend Intensives

Compact training weekends built around skating edges, angles, tracking, and recovery habits.

Small Groups

Low-ratio goalie groups that give each player more shots, corrections, and coach attention.

Preseason Prep

Structured work before the season starts, with tempo, conditioning, and game-situation habits.

Tryout Readiness

Focused sessions for confidence, crease movement, and decision-making before evaluations.

Video Review

Simple review sessions to connect on-ice habits with game clips and clear next steps.
