About Goalie Academy

Goalie Academy helps young goalies develop through regular, structured, and easy-to-understand training. The academy offers different formats for U7-U20 players in Helsinki.

  • Individual training
  • Mini-group sessions
  • Group training
  • Camps and intensives
  • Location: Helsinki / Salmisaari Arena

Training Method

The training method is built around strong technical fundamentals, repeated quality reps, and game-ready goalie habits. Each session is adapted to the player's age, level, and current development needs.

  • Skating and movement
  • Positioning and angles
  • Puck tracking and reactions
  • Recovery techniques
  • Game situations and decision-making

Academy Philosophy

Goalie Academy believes in patient development, confidence through repetition, and long-term progress. The atmosphere is positive, demanding, and focused on helping every goalie trust the process.

  • Patient development
  • Confidence through repetition
  • Discipline and consistency
  • Mistakes as part of learning
  • Long-term progress over shortcuts
